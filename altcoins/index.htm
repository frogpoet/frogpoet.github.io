Select the slug button for any altcoin below to vote for it to be included in the next edition of the selected month's Altcoin Investigation. These are altcoins which reached the milestone of being listed on CoinMarketCap® during the selected month. If the selected month is the current month, then editions come out daily, if possible.

Note that you can vote for as many coins as you want, as many times as you like, to accurately reflect your desire to receive information about the coin. In this context, information means :

  1. business problems which the coin is meant to help solve according to official documentation of the coin
  2. scam accusations and responses to those accusations buried deep within forum pages
Information about business problems include links to particular pages of whitepapers and quotes from them which describe how usage of the coin would go about helping to solve or lessen them. Information about scam accusations and responses to those accusations include links pointing to specific forum pages so that readers may judge for themselves whether to believe the accusations. The intent of this information is to help speed the process of investigating altcoins in terms of what good they provide to society and the general public perception of how trustworthy the coin is - the author makes no actual claims of whether a coin is a scam or not, or is worthwhile or not; he merely provides information in an objective, fair manner, so that the reader can make his or her own decision.

Note that there will probably be a 24-hour delay between the casting of votes and resulting additions to the next edition. Only altcoins listed on this page receiving the highest number of votes will be the ones included, due to the author's time consraints. If an altcoin slug button is disabled and the row has a grey background, then research on the altcoin has already been included in the book. If an altcoin button is disabled and the row has a bluish background, then research on the altcoin is currently in process.
