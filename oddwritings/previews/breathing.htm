Preview : Breathing in Quarantine

Recollections of a Nation

those who breathe
conspire
rekindling dying fires
so that one remembers
youth
red in claw and tooth.

those who touch
conceal
the revelations that they feel
so that one repaints a
fence
white with innocence.

those who spy
convince
with revolutions that they glimpse
so that one respects his
dead
blue for having bled.
