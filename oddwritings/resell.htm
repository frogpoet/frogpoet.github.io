How to Resell NFTs you have Collected on OddWritings.cent.co

  1. Log into Cent
  2. Go to my page
  3. Collect the NFT
  4. Wait for all of the NFTs to be collected
  5. Make a note of the title of the NFT (select and copy it to the clipboard)
  6. Make sure you have an account and wallet on OpenSea with which you can log in
  7. Log into OpenSea by connecting your wallet
  8. Search for the NFT by pasting the title into the search bar. If found, select it. If not found, you need to wait longer for OpenSea to detect it
  9. Note the prices so you have an idea what you want to sell yours for.
  10. Select your wallet icon and then select your address to copy it to the clipboard
  11. Go back to your account on Cent
  12. View your collection and select OddWritings as the creator
  13. If you don't see the NFT you just collected, refresh the screen (F5 on windows desktop)
  14. Select the "Transfer NFT to external wallet" option on that NFT
  15. Paste in your wallet address and Transfer
  16. Go back to your account on OpenSea and view your profile
  17. Go to the "Hidden" section (it might be under More...)
  18. If you don't see the NFT you just transferred, refresh the screen (F5 on windows desktop). You may need to wait longer for OpenSea to detect it
  19. Select the options on that NFT and unhide it
  20. Go to your Collected items
  21. Select the NFT you just unhid and choose Sell.
