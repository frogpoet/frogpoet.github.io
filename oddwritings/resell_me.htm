How to Resell the NFT you have Just Collected on OddWritings.cent.co

If you collected the NFT from OddWritings.cent.co, follow these steps:

  1. Make sure you have an account and wallet on OpenSea with which you can log in. Go here for help.
  2. Log into OpenSea by connecting your wallet
  3. Select your address to copy it to the clipboard
  4. Go back to Cent (you should already be logged in)
  5. View your collection and select OddWritings as the creator
  6. Select the "Transfer NFT to external wallet" option on the NFT you have just collected (they are two arrows pointing horizontally)
  7. Paste in your wallet address and Transfer. Wait for the 'Successfully Transferred' message to appear.
  8. Go back to OpenSea, go to Account, and go to Profile
  9. Go to the "Hidden" section (it might be under More...)
  10. If you don't see the NFT you just transferred, refresh the screen (F5 on windows desktop). You may need to wait up to 48 hours for OpenSea to detect it
  11. Select the options on that NFT (the 3 dots) and unhide it. Click continue (you may need to tell your wallet to accept this action)
  12. Go to your Collected items
  13. Select the options on that NFT and choose Sell.
If you collected the NFT from OpenSea, follow these steps:
  1. Go to your Collected items
  2. Select the options on that NFT (the 3 dots) and choose Sell.
