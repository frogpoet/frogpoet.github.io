Odd Writings

I like writing odd things.

Every Musician's Nightmare

cracked the ancient evergreen
from wood-stock to his crown.
one too many rock-and-roll
disasters brought him down.

sycamores and sycophants
had gathered all around
to dress his wounds with lathered leaves
in aloe vera bound.

"call the medics! get some help!"
the king of music frowned;
his bandages were stinging
as he fell upon the ground.

"what is your emergency?"
the nurse they would astound
for now the curse of forests struck
and no one made a sound.
