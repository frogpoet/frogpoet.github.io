Odd Writings

I like writing odd things.

I'm currently working on 'Cressida', a group of poems on Leanpub. One of the things i like about leanpub is that it lets you sell your draft manuscript as you are writing it. People who buy it will then get updates to the draft for free whenever you republish the draft - this allows them to send you anonymous email on the site with their feedback on your work so far.

I've thought that i could provide a sort of advertising service for people that did that with my draft. Say you are a poet who is trying to sell your independently published work, and you also like buying poetry from time to time. You could do both things at once by buying my draft. This is because if you buy my draft and then email me on leanpub with your transaction id, name, and the title of your book, i will add your name and book title to my acknowledgements page for the poets who have reviewed my manuscript. This page appears towards the front of the book, so even people who 'peek inside' the book once it is complete and distributed to amazon, apple iBooks, etc. will see your name and title while doing so. Then i will add a "further reading" section at the end of the book, where i will again put your name and title, as well as an image of the front cover of your book which i will find on the web and scale accordingly to match with the other images in the "further reading" section.

There is a free pdf sample that you can download of Cressida so you can see what i'm talking about, and so you can get a feel for my poetry, by clicking below and then clicking on the 'read free sample' button.

The price above is half of the eventual price, since the document is in draft form. Once i reach 250 pages to complete it, i will start selling it at the normal price, as well as create print, epub, and kindle versions to distribute everywhere i can where books are sold online. The acknowledgements page and the further reading section will appear in all versions of course, so this would be like an advertisement for your book which never goes away.

There are other poetry books that i have completed, though none of them have used the above advertising idea. I have included them below with places where you can find them.
