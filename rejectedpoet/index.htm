Welcome to the Page of the Rejected Poet

CC0 Image by Willgard Krause (user:Willgard) on Pixabay

Bio: I hope to make an income by selling poems. I have a college degree and have been published in SkywayJournal (2021 - February), Open Skies Quarterly (Volume 4), and Writes of the Round Table (ed. Lucie Sargeant).

Literary Concoctions: These are what I like to call collections in which not all of the content consists of poems. Sometimes the extra content will contain humorous tracts or will be word games, for example; but whatever the non-poetic material is, it will be creative. I also term these things 'fragments' when space is limited, such as on book covers.
