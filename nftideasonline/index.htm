NFT Ideas Online

I like thinking of ways to use Non-Fungible Tokens to solve societal problems.

The purpose of this website is to share ideas I come up with involving the use of NFTs to solve problems. These are ideas that I believe have not yet been implemented by any organizations or companies - if you know of an organization or company that already offers a solution, do let me know at contact@nftideas.online and i will gladly mention the company or organization in the appropriate section. Each time that I come up with an idea, I tweet about it on my @NftIdeas twitter account and tag an individual whom I believe would be helpful in either implementing the idea or supporting it or spreading it, and then I immediately create an NFT of that tweet on the Valuables web site. What I want to do is see these ideas come to fruition due to the efforts of other people (with myself getting involved if needed, though I would prefer to keep my laid-back lifestyle), as I tend to have more ideas than I can attempt to implement myself, and I would rather they be implemented by SOMEBODY rather than being lost in the fog of my memory.

I have subdivided the ideas into categories, which follow. You can jump from category to category using the menu at the top of your screen, with the 'Examples' dropdown.
